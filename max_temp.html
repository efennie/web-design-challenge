Latitude and Maximum Temperature

This graph shows the relationship between latitude and maximum temperature.
The realtionship seems is connected. The graph clearly displays
a cluster of data points increasing as we get closer to the equator and
and decreasing as we move away from the equator in either direction.
If we were to continue to pull weather data over a longer span of time
we would expect this relationship to continue to show a relationship between
temperature and latitude.
